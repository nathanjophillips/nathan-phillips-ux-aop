---
title: 'File Organization'
---

```
[dist]
[node_modules]
[src]
  [sass]
  [js]
    [controllers]
    [directives]
    [services]
    module.js
    routes.js
  [partials]
  index.html
[test]
    [unit]
    [e2e]
    karma.conf.js
    protractor.conf.js
.editorconfig
.gitignore
.jshintrc
gulpfile.js
package.json
README.md
```
- `[name]` = directories
- `name.fileType` or `.fileType` = files
